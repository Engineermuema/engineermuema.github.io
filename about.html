About Legacy FC

Legacy FC was founded in 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic. Since then, the club has impacted the lives of many youths in Athi River through football, mentorship, and guidance. At Legacy FC, we believe in not only playing football but also growing spiritually and building a strong foundation for life.

Our mission is to foster talent, promote sportsmanship, and create a lasting impact in the community. We strive to build a legacy that transcends the game and inspires greatness in everyone we touch.

With determination and unity, we are optimistic that we will win the Mavoko Sub County League and continue to inspire greatness in our community.
